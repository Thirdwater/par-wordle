What is Par-Wordle?

Par-Wordle is an extension to the Wordle game that displays a score showing how many possible words are left, given all the previous guesses.

Why?

One idealized way of playing Wordle is by eliminating all other possibilities first before deducing the answer, thus achieving the "par-0" score.

In a sense, this score is a measure of how lucky we are when we reach the answer, and we may want to minimize this number.
